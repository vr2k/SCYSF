Honk! Noise workshop hosted by Simon Clark

An online noise workshop exploring the fringes of voice. We’ll test the capacity of mouth-sounds beyond the conventions of speech, inviting our lungs, larynxes and vocal folds into strange new contortions that break the habit of making sense. The mortifications of digital video conferencing will be emphatically embraced as a creative constraint.
